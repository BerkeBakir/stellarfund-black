The problem: creators outside major financial hubs can't access Kickstarter/GoFundMe
payout rails, and backers have no guarantee their money is used as promised.

StellarFund fixes both with on-chain **milestone escrow**.

**3/**
How it works:
• Back a campaign in XLM
• Funds sit in a Soroban escrow contract — not a middleman
• Each milestone releases one tranche to the creator
• Miss the goal? Every backer is refunded by code

**4/**
Architecture: a Factory deploys a dedicated escrow per campaign, and a Reputation
contract tracks creators who actually deliver.
